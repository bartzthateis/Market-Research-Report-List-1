<p><p>The composites piezoelectric material market is categorized into several types based on the combination of piezoelectric phases. The type 1-3 composites involve the integration of one-dimensional piezoelectric fibers within a second-phase polymer matrix, enhancing mechanical properties and energy conversion efficiency. In contrast, the type 2-2 composites consist of a two-dimensional arrangement with piezoelectric ceramics interleaved with polymers, offering improved flexibility and durability. These classifications cater to diverse applications in sensors, actuators, and energy harvesting devices.</p></p>
