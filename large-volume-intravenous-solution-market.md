<p><strong>The Large Volume Intravenous Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalloids</li><li>Colloids</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large volume intravenous solution market comprises three primary types: crystalloids, colloids, and others. Crystalloids are solutions of electrolytes and non-electrolytes that easily pass through cell membranes, commonly used for hydration and electrolyte balance. Colloids consist of larger molecules, which help maintain osmotic pressure and are used in volume expansion and blood volume restoration. The "others" category includes specialized solutions tailored for specific medical conditions, such as nutrient-rich formulations or medications mixed for specific treatments.</p></p>
