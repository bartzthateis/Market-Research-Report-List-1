<p><p>The industrial furnace market is primarily divided into two types: combustion-type and electric-type furnaces. Combustion-type industrial furnaces utilize fossil fuels like natural gas or oil to generate heat, making them suitable for high-temperature processes and large-scale operations. In contrast, electric-type industrial furnaces use electric energy to achieve heating, offering greater temperature control and efficiency, particularly in environments where precise temperatures are crucial. Both types serve diverse industries, including metallurgy, ceramics, and glass manufacturing.</p></p>
