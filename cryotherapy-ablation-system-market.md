<p><p>The Cryotherapy Ablation System market consists of various technologies, including Phase Change Refrigeration and Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration. Phase Change Refrigeration utilizes materials that absorb heat during phase transitions, effectively cooling targeted tissues for ablation. In contrast, Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration relies on the expansion of gas, resulting in a temperature drop that aids in therapeutic cooling. Both methods are essential for precise tissue destruction while minimizing damage to surrounding structures, making them crucial in medical applications.</p></p>
