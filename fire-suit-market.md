<p><p>The fire suit market is categorized into two main types: coats and pants. Fire coats are designed to protect the upper body of firefighters from heat, flames, and hazardous materials. They typically feature insulation, reinforced seams, and moisture barriers. Fire pants, on the other hand, protect the lower body, combining similar safety features like thermal protection and abrasion resistance. Both components are essential for ensuring comprehensive safety and mobility during firefighting operations, ultimately enabling effective emergency response.</p></p>
