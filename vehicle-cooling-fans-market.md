<p><strong>The Vehicle Cooling Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Radiator Fan</li><li>Electric Radiator Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle cooling fans market is primarily segmented into mechanical and electric radiator fans. Mechanical radiator fans are driven by the engine's rotation, utilizing a belt system to regulate airflow based on engine temperature. Conversely, electric radiator fans operate independently of the engine and are powered by electricity, allowing for more precise control and better efficiency. The choice between these fan types often depends on vehicle design, performance requirements, and fuel efficiency priorities. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal engine temperatures.</p></p>
