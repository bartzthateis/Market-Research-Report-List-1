<p><p>The Commercial and Industrial Steam Humidifiers market features two primary types: Electrode Type and Resistive Type. Electrode Type humidifiers use electric currents passing through water to generate steam, offering rapid response and efficient humidity control. In contrast, Resistive Type humidifiers rely on heating elements to warm water, producing steam. While both types serve to enhance humidity in large spaces, they differ in energy efficiency, maintenance requirements, and initial costs, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
