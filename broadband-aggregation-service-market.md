<p><ul><li>Digital Subscriber Line (DSL) Service</li><li>Passive Optical Network (PON) Service</li><li>Ethernet FTTH Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Broadband Aggregation Service encompasses various technologies for delivering high-speed internet. Digital Subscriber Line (DSL) Service utilizes existing telephone lines to provide internet access, prioritizing distance from the provider for speed. Passive Optical Network (PON) Service employs fiber optic cables to distribute data through passive splitters, enabling efficient, high-capacity connections. Ethernet FTTH (Fiber to the Home) Service leverages point-to-point fiber optic links, offering high-speed internet directly to homes, ensuring superior bandwidth and low latency, thus catering to diverse user demands.</p></p>
