<p><strong>The Reflective Sheeting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bead Type</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflective sheeting market primarily consists of two types: glass bead type and micro prismatic type. Glass bead sheeting utilizes spherical glass beads embedded in a polymer layer to reflect light, offering moderate visibility and durability, commonly used in signs and markings. Micro prismatic sheeting, on the other hand, incorporates a series of microscopic prisms that enhance brightness and visibility, especially in low-light conditions. This advanced technology is often preferred for high-visibility applications, providing superior performance for traffic signs and safety markings.</p></p>
