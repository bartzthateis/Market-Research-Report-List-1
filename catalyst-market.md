<p><p>Catalyst market types include zeolites, metals, chemical compounds, enzymes, and organometallic materials, each serving distinct roles in chemical reactions. Zeolites are porous minerals used in petrochemical processes. Metals act as powerful catalysts in various reactions, typically in their nanoparticle forms. Chemical compounds encompass a range of substances that facilitate specific reactions. Enzymes are biological catalysts that enhance reaction rates in biochemical processes. Organometallic materials combine organic and inorganic components, offering unique catalytic properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
