<p><p>The observability tool market encompasses three primary types: metrics, logs, and traces. Metrics provide quantitative data about system performance, enabling teams to monitor key indicators over time. Logs capture detailed event information, assisting in troubleshooting and understanding application behavior. Traces track the flow of requests through distributed systems, offering insights into latency and bottlenecks. Together, these elements create a comprehensive picture of system health, facilitating effective monitoring, debugging, and optimization for complex applications.</p></p>
