<p><strong>The Nepheline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nepheline Syenite</li><li>Nepheline Monzonite</li><li>Nephelinites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nepheline market encompasses three primary types: Nepheline Syenite, Nepheline Monzonite, and Nephelinites. Nepheline Syenite is prized for its use in ceramics and glass industries due to its low thermal expansion and high alumina content. Nepheline Monzonite, with a balanced composition of feldspar and nepheline, is utilized in construction and as a raw material in various industrial processes. Nephelinites, characterized by their high nepheline content, have applications in refractory materials, fertilizers, and as a mineral filler, expanding their industrial utility.</p></p>
