<p><p>Heparin-Induced Thrombocytopenia (HIT) treatment market encompasses various diagnostic assays for identifying HIT. The Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) detects antibodies against heparin-platelet complexes, while the Serotonin Release Assay (SRA) measures serotonin release from platelets upon exposure to heparin. The Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is used for antibody detection as well. The Heparin-Induced Platelet Activation (HIPA) Test evaluates platelet activation in response to heparin, aiding in diagnosing HIT and guiding effective treatment strategies.</p></p>
