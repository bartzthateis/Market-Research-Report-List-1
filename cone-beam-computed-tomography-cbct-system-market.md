<p><p>Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) is a specialized imaging technique designed primarily for dental and maxillofacial applications. Unlike traditional CT, CBCT uses a cone-shaped X-ray beam to capture three-dimensional images of dental structures, soft tissues, nerve paths, and the skeletal system in a single scan, offering enhanced visualization with lower radiation exposure. This technology improves diagnostic accuracy, treatment planning, and outcomes in various dental procedures, such as implants, orthognathic surgery, and endodontics.</p><p>The CBCT system market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced imaging technologies in dentistry, rising awareness of oral health, and the growing prevalence of dental diseases. Additionally, the ongoing technological advancements in CBCT systems, including enhanced software for image analysis, are further propelling market expansion. The growing trend of integrating CBCT with digital dentistry and surgical planning software is also noteworthy. The Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period, reflecting the ongoing investment and innovation in this field, which is essential for improving treatment efficacy and patient satisfaction in dental care.</p></p>
