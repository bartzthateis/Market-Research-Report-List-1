<p><p>The UV fluorescent inks market comprises two main types: UV invisible fluorescent ink and UV visible fluorescent ink. UV invisible fluorescent ink is designed to be invisible under normal light but glows brightly under UV light, making it ideal for security applications, such as anti-counterfeiting measures. In contrast, UV visible fluorescent ink is bright and vibrant under both natural and UV light, often used in advertising, decorative printing, and packaging to enhance visual appeal and attract consumer attention.</p></p>
