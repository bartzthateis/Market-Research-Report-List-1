<p><ul><li>Open Loop Motor</li><li>Closed Loop Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone camera VCM (Voice Coil Motor) market comprises two primary types: open-loop and closed-loop motors. Open-loop motors operate without feedback, simplifying design and reducing costs but may have slight inaccuracies in positioning. In contrast, closed-loop motors utilize feedback mechanisms to ensure precise control and adjustment, enhancing image quality and focusing speed. These distinctions influence manufacturers' choices based on performance requirements, cost considerations, and the desired level of image stabilization and focusing accuracy in mobile devices.</p></p>
