<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Sprayer</li><li>Worm-drive Pump Sprayers</li><li>Peristaltic Pump Sprayers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plaster spraying machines are categorized into several types based on their mechanism and application. Pneumatic sprayers utilize compressed air to atomize and spray plaster, providing a smooth finish. Worm-drive pump sprayers employ a gear-driven system to ensure consistent material flow, ideal for thicker applications. Peristaltic pump sprayers operate by compressing flexible tubes, offering precise control over the material's delivery. Other market types may include electric sprayers and hydraulic systems, catering to various project scales and plaster types.</p></p>
