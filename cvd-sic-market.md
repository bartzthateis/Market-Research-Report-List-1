<p><p>The CVD SiC (Chemical Vapor Deposition Silicon Carbide) market is segmented into three resistivity grades: high, middle, and low. High resistivity grade SiC is used in applications requiring excellent electrical insulation, such as in high-voltage devices. Middle resistivity grade SiC balances conductivity and insulation, making it suitable for general electronic applications. Low resistivity grade SiC is designed for high-conductivity applications, including power electronics and various semiconductor devices, where efficiency and heat management are critical.</p></p>
