<p><p>The microwave absorber sheet and panel market is categorized into low permeability and high permeability types. Low permeability absorbers are designed to limit electromagnetic wave penetration, effectively reflecting and dissipating microwaves, making them ideal for shielding applications. In contrast, high permeability absorbers allow greater microwave passage while still reducing reflections, suitable for applications requiring optimal signal transmission. Both types are crucial in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace, where managing microwave interference is essential for performance and safety.</p></p>
