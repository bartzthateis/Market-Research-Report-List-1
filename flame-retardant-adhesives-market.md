<p><p>The flame retardant adhesives market is categorized into two main types: halogen-free flame retardant adhesives and halogen flame retardant adhesives. Halogen-free flame retardant adhesives utilize non-halogenated substances to reduce flammability, making them more environmentally friendly and safer for health. In contrast, halogen flame retardant adhesives incorporate halogen compounds, which are highly effective in preventing ignition but have raised environmental and health concerns. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and electronics for improved safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
