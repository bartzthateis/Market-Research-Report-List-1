<p><p>The polypropylene pipes market consists of three main types: PP-R/RCT, PP-H, and PP-B. PP-R/RCT pipes are known for their excellent thermal properties and are used in hot and cold water systems. PP-H pipes are characterized by high impact resistance and are typically used in industrial applications. PP-B pipes, with their superior chemical resistance, are ideal for conveying aggressive media in various industries. Each type offers distinct benefits suited for specific applications in plumbing, construction, and chemical transport.</p></p>
