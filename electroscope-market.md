<p><p>The electroscope market can be categorized primarily into two types: the Pith-Ball electroscope and the Gold-Leaf electroscope. The Pith-Ball electroscope utilizes lightweight pith balls suspended on a string, which move apart when charged, indicating the presence of electric charge. In contrast, the Gold-Leaf electroscope features delicate gold leaves that separate when influenced by a charge. Both devices serve educational and experimental purposes in demonstrating electric charge principles, appealing to schools, laboratories, and science enthusiasts.</p></p>
