<p><p>Automatic Tridimensional Storehouses encompass various market types designed for efficient storage and retrieval. Unit Load systems handle large pallets, while Mini Load caters to smaller items with high-density storage. Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) use vertical space for compact storage and quick access. Carousels offer rotational storage for items, enhancing retrieval speed. Mid Load systems balance between unit and mini for versatile applications. Autostore utilizes grid systems for maximum space efficiency and automated picking, optimizing warehouse operations.</p></p>
