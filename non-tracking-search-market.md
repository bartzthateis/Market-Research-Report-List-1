<p><p>Non-tracking search encompasses two main market types: search engines and meta-search engines. Search engines, like Google, directly index and rank webpages based on user queries, focusing on delivering relevant content without tracking user behavior. Meta-search engines, such as DuckDuckGo, aggregate results from multiple search engines, providing a broader range of options while maintaining user privacy. Both types prioritize user anonymity, avoiding the collection of personal data, which appeals to individuals seeking privacy in their online searches.</p></p>
