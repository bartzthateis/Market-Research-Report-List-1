<p><p>The connected street light market comprises two main types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems utilize Power Line Communication (PLC) to transmit data through existing electrical wiring, ensuring reliable connectivity and lower latency. In contrast, wireless systems leverage technologies like Wi-Fi, Zigbee, or cellular networks to offer flexibility in installation and scalability. While wired solutions often provide stable connections, wireless systems allow for easier retrofitting and adaptability to changing urban environments, catering to diverse smart city needs.</p></p>
