<p><p>ESD foam packaging is essential for protecting sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge. There are several types: Conductive foam is made from conductive polymers or metals, providing a path for electrical charge to dissipate safely. Dissipative foam, on the other hand, features a controlled resistance that allows for gradual charge dissipation. Additionally, additive-based foam incorporates ESD additives, enhancing the packaging's protective qualities without compromising physical properties. Together, these types address various needs in the ESD-sensitive market.</p></p>
