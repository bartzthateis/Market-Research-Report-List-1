<p><strong>The Indoor Optical Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Double Cores</li><li>Multi Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor optical cable market comprises three main types: single core, double core, and multi cores. Single core cables feature one optical fiber strand, suitable for straightforward applications and short-distance transmission. Double core cables include two strands, enhancing redundancy and allowing for bi-directional communication. Multi core cables, housing multiple fibers, support high-capacity data transfers, ideal for complex setups and extensive networks. Each type serves distinct needs in telecommunications, data centers, and building infrastructure, facilitating efficient and reliable connectivity.</p></p>
