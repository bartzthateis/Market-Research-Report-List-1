<p><p>The Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) device market includes various types designed for diagnosis and treatment. Esophagoscopy and gastroscopy devices visualize the esophagus and stomach. Fundoplication devices assist in surgical procedures to prevent acid reflux. Manometry systems measure esophageal pressure, while pH monitoring systems assess acid levels. Radiofrequency ablation devices target and eliminate abnormal tissue. Sphincter augmentation devices help reinforce the lower esophageal sphincter, and ultrasonic surgical endostaplers facilitate precise surgical closures. Each device plays a vital role in managing GERD.</p></p>
