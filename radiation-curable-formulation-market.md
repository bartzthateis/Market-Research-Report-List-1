<p><strong>The Radiation Curable Formulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing</li><li>Electron beam (EB) Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation curable formulation market includes two primary types: Ultraviolet (UV) curing and Electron Beam (EB) curing. UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to trigger polymerization, rapidly solidifying inks, coatings, and adhesives, making it ideal for applications requiring quick-drying processes. Conversely, EB curing employs high-energy electrons to achieve similar results, providing deeper penetration and less reliance on photoinitiators, which enhances durability and reduces environmental impact. Both technologies cater to diverse industries like printing, coatings, and electronics.</p></p>
