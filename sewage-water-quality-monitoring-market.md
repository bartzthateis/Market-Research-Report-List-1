<p><ul><li>Multi-parameter Measurement</li><li>Single Parameter Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sewage water quality monitoring encompasses two main market types: multi-parameter measurement and single-parameter measurement. Multi-parameter measurement devices assess several water quality indicators simultaneously, providing a comprehensive overview of sewage conditions. This method is efficient for capturing dynamic changes and overall water quality. In contrast, single-parameter measurement focuses on one specific indicator, such as pH or dissolved oxygen. This approach is often used for detailed analysis or compliance verification, allowing for targeted assessments in sewage treatment processes.</p></p>
