<p><p>The colored lip balm market is categorized into two main types: liquid lip balm and solid lip balm. Liquid lip balms offer a lightweight, glossy finish, often providing a more vibrant color payoff and hydration. They are typically packaged in tubes or applicators for easy application. Solid lip balms, on the other hand, are wax-based and provide a more substantial texture with a matte or satin finish. They focus on long-lasting hydration and color, often found in traditional stick form. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
