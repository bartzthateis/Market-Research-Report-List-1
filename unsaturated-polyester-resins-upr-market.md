<p><p>Unsaturated Polyester Resins (UPR) are versatile materials widely used in the production of composite materials. The market is primarily divided into two types: Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) and Non-FRP applications. FRP utilizes UPR combined with reinforcing fibers to create strong, lightweight composites for construction, automotive, and marine industries. In contrast, the Non-FRP market employs UPR in applications like coatings, adhesives, and moldings, where high strength is less critical. Both segments benefit from UPR's durability and flexibility in various applications.</p></p>
